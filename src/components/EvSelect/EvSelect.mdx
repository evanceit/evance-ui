### Affix to items with `items-prefix` &amp; `items-suffix` slots
The list items within the menu may be prefixed and/or suffixed
using the `items-prefix` and `items-suffix` slots respectively.

Both are included within the scroll of the list, if you would like
your components to appear outside the scrollable area of the list
you may use the `list-prefix` and/or `list-suffix` slots.

<Canvas of={EvSelectStories.ItemsAffix} />

<br />

### Affix to list with `list-prefix` &amp; `list-suffix` slots
The scrollable list of items may be prefixed and/or suffixed
using the `list-prefix` and `list-suffix` slots respectively.

Both exist outside the scrollable area of the list. If you would like
your components to appear inside the scrollable list area you may use
the `items-prefix` and/or `items-suffix` slots.

### Custom `item` &amp; `selection` rendering
List items and selected items may be customized using the `item` and `selection` slot respectively.

The `item` slot receives `item`, `index` and `props` variables and requires a `<ev-list-item v-bind="props">`
element as the root child of the template. However, you may customize the list item as required or
render your own subcomponent based on the `item.raw` data originally supplied to the select box.

```html
<template #item="{item, index, props}">
    <ev-list-item v-bind="props">
        // use item.raw to render your custom item
    </ev-list-item>
</template>
```

The `selection` slot receives `item` and `index` variables allowing you to customise the look
of selected items.

```html
<template #selection="{item, index}">
    // use item.raw to render your custom selection
</template>
```
